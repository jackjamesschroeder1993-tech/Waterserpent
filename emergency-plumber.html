24/7 Emergency Plumbing Fast Response, Day or Night

Plumbing emergencies do not happen at a convenient hour. Burst pipes, sewer overflows, blocked toilets in the middle of the night, hot water leaks and gas leaks are all genuine emergencies that need a plumber on site fast — not in two days.

Water Serpent Plumbing runs a 24/7 emergency line with real plumbers, not a call centre. We aim for one to two hour response across our core eastern suburbs service area, day, night, weekend or public holiday.

Common Warning Signs

  • Burst pipe inside the home: isolate the water at the meter and call us immediately — every minute counts.
  • Sewer overflow or raw sewage in the home: a serious health hazard requiring same-hour response.
  • No hot water in winter: we carry spares for the most common brands for fast restoration.
  • Smell of gas indoors: leave the property, do not switch anything on or off, call your gas supplier and then us.
  • Roof leaks into the ceiling cavity: we isolate the leak source and prevent ceiling collapse.
  • Blocked toilet (only one in the house): cleared same-visit, day or night.

Our Diagnostic & Repair Process

  1. Immediate triage: a plumber speaks with you to help isolate water or gas and protect property.
  2. Rapid dispatch: the nearest on-call van is dispatched with parts to handle 90% of common emergencies on the spot.
  3. Containment first: we stop the damage — isolate water, cap leaks, vacuum standing water.
  4. Permanent fix or temporary make-safe: whichever is appropriate; we never push unnecessary works during an emergency.
  5. Insurance documentation: photos, written report and cause analysis ready for your insurer.

Equipment We Use

  • Fully stocked emergency vans: pipe, fittings, valves, hot water spares, isolation tools.
  • Wet-vacs & containment kit: for water mitigation while we repair.
  • Leak detection gear: on board 24/7 for hidden leaks in walls and slabs.
  • Gas testers: certified equipment for safe gas leak isolation.

Service Area Across Melbourne's Eastern Suburbs

We service Ringwood, Bayswater, Croydon, Ferntree Gully, Boronia, Wantirna, Kilsyth, Mooroolbark, Glen Waverley, Lilydale and surrounding eastern and outer-eastern Melbourne suburbs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you get to me?

Most calls within our core eastern suburbs service area are attended within 60–120 minutes, 24 hours a day.

Is there an after-hours surcharge?

Our after-hours rates are clearly disclosed before we dispatch. There are no hidden charges and we provide a written quote on arrival.

What should I do while I wait?

Turn off the water at the meter for any leak, turn off the power at the switchboard if water is near electrics, and clear valuables from the affected area.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes. We provide insurer-ready written reports, cause-of-damage documentation and full photo evidence.
