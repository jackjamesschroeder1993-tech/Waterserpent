Common Blocked Drain Causes in Chirnside Park

Chirnside Park's mix of newer estates, established pockets and flat creek-flat blocks produces a predictable set of drainage problems. The most common blocked drain causes we see are:

  • Pipe sag and bellies in newer-estate PVC sewer lines caused by ground settlement and poor bedding
  • Construction silt, sand and debris left in younger drain lines
  • Grease, soap and food waste accumulation in family residential kitchen and bathroom drains
  • Stormwater pit and pipe overload on flat, low-lying blocks near the Yarra flats and Olinda Creek
  • Tree root intrusion into the earthenware sewer lines of the older 1970s–80s streets
  • Hair, soap and wet wipes blocking bathroom, shower and toilet lines

Pipe Relining & Sagging-Pipe Repair in Chirnside Park

In Chirnside Park's older streets, repeat blockages usually mean roots returning through the same joint — pipe relining fixes that for good by sealing an epoxy liner inside the existing pipe to create a smooth, root-resistant surface, without excavating your garden. In the newer estates the problem is more often a sagging or bellied section of PVC where waste pools and blocks; we use the CCTV camera to pinpoint the exact spot and depth so any repair is targeted, not guesswork.
