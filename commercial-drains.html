Commercial Plumbing & Property Manager Services Reliable, Documented, Insured

Water Serpent Plumbing partners with real estate agencies, owners corporations, body corporates, retail tenancies, hospitality venues and facility managers across Melbourne's east. We understand the commercial reality: tenants need a fast response, owners need clean documentation, and managers need a contractor who turns up when they say they will.

Every job we do for a managed property comes with a same-day photo-supported report, an itemised invoice ready for owner approval, and a workmanship guarantee in writing.

Common Service Calls

  • Tenant maintenance requests: blocked drains, leaking taps, hot water failures, toilet repairs.
  • Owners-corp common-area issues: stack blockages, basement pump failures, riser leaks, downpipe overflows.
  • Hospitality venue grease lines: kitchen drains, grease traps, scheduled hydro-jet maintenance.
  • Retail tenancy fit-outs: compliance plumbing for new tenancies, certifications and lodgements.
  • Compliance & insurance reporting: CCTV reports, photo logs and certifications for insurers and inspections.

Our Service Process

  1. Single-point coordination: one contact, one job number, one invoice — no chasing.
  2. Tenant liaison: we contact tenants directly within an agreed window to book access.
  3. Documented works: before/after photos and a written summary delivered same day.
  4. Owner-approval-ready quotes: fixed-price quotes formatted for fast owner approval.
  5. Scheduled maintenance: we set up recurring hydro-jetting and inspection schedules for hospitality and high-rise properties.

Equipment We Use

  • Trailer-mounted jetters: commercial-grade equipment for retail, hospitality and high-rise.
  • Tractor CCTV cameras: large-diameter stack and main inspections up to 300mm.
  • Confined-space accreditation: pits, basements and tank entries handled in-house.
  • 24/7 on-call team: after-hours emergencies covered, every day of the year.

Service Area Across Melbourne's Eastern Suburbs

We service Ringwood, Bayswater, Croydon, Ferntree Gully, Boronia, Wantirna, Kilsyth, Mooroolbark, Glen Waverley, Lilydale and surrounding eastern Melbourne suburbs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. We invoice the agency or the owner directly per your preference, with itemised line items and supporting evidence.

We aim for two-hour response on emergencies and same-day or next-business-day on routine maintenance, within our core service area.
