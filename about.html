Why We Focus on Blocked Drains

Blocked drains are one of the most frustrating and disruptive plumbing problems a homeowner faces. They always seem to happen at the worst time, and too often, plumbers apply a quick fix that doesn't address the real cause. Jack got tired of seeing homeowners pay twice for jobs that weren't done right the first time. Water Serpent Plumbing was built to fix that.

We invest in proper equipment — CCTV drain cameras, 5000 PSI hydro jetters, pipe locating tools — because the right equipment makes the difference between a proper fix and a temporary band-aid.

Our Equipment

  • 5000 PSI high-pressure water jetter — the most powerful clearing method available
  • CCTV drain camera — inspect and record exactly what's happening inside your pipes
  • Pipe locating equipment — find buried pipes without unnecessary digging
  • Full diagnostic tools for accurate assessment every time
